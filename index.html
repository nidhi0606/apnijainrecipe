Weight-Loss Style Tofu & Walnut Rolls

Ingredients:

  • 200g tofu, crumbled
  • 2 tbsp walnuts, chopped
  • 1 tsp olive oil
  • Salt & pepper to taste
  • 1 tsp soy sauce (optional)

Instructions:

  1. Mix tofu and walnuts in a bowl with salt, pepper, and olive oil.
  2. Form small rolls or patties.
  3. Heat a non-stick pan and cook each roll for 3–4 minutes per side until golden.
  4. Serve hot with a side salad.

Pumpkin Seed Cabbage Rolls

Ingredients:

  • 6 large cabbage leaves
  • 1 cup cooked quinoa or rice
  • 2 tbsp pumpkin seeds
  • 1 carrot, grated
  • Salt, pepper, and herbs

Instructions:

  1. Blanch cabbage leaves until soft.
  2. Mix cooked quinoa, pumpkin seeds, carrot, and seasoning.
  3. Fill each leaf with the mixture and roll tightly.
  4. Steam or bake for 10 minutes before serving.

Himachali–Uttarakhandi Tofu Stuffed Shimla Mirch

Ingredients:

  • 4 bell peppers
  • 150g tofu, crumbled
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 1 tsp cumin seeds
  • Salt, pepper, and garam masala

Instructions:

  1. Cut tops of bell peppers and remove seeds.
  2. Sauté onion, tofu, and spices.
  3. Stuff bell peppers with the mixture.
  4. Bake at 180°C for 20 minutes.

Cabbage Tofu Rolls with Sweet Corn & Walnut Twist

Ingredients:

  • 6 cabbage leaves
  • 100g tofu, crumbled
  • ½ cup sweet corn
  • 2 tbsp walnuts, chopped
  • Salt, pepper, herbs

Instructions:

  1. Blanch cabbage leaves until soft.
  2. Mix tofu, sweet corn, walnuts, and seasoning.
  3. Fill cabbage leaves with the mixture and roll.
  4. Steam or bake for 10 minutes before serving.
