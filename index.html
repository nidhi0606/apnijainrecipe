Top 5 Delicious Jain Recipes

Tofu Stir Fry

Ingredients: Tofu, bell peppers, soy sauce, garlic, ginger.

Instructions: Cut tofu and vegetables, sauté with garlic and ginger, add soy sauce, serve hot.

Cabbage Rolls

Ingredients: Cabbage, rice, carrots, beans, tomato sauce.

Instructions: Boil cabbage leaves, fill with rice and veggies, bake with tomato sauce.

Vegetable Pulao

Ingredients: Basmati rice, peas, carrots, beans, cumin, ghee.

Instructions: Sauté spices and vegetables, add rice and water, cook until done.

Paneer Butter Masala

Ingredients: Paneer, tomato puree, butter, cream, spices.

Instructions: Cook tomato puree with spices, add paneer and cream, simmer and serve.

Methi Thepla

Ingredients: Whole wheat flour, fenugreek leaves (methi), spices, oil.

Instructions: Mix dough with methi and spices, roll, cook on skillet until golden.
